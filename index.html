About us

We understand the Organization Structures specific to an Infrastructure Company, Project Work Flow, and Job profiles like BD, Tendering & Bidding, Design, Planning, SCM & Procurement, Construction, Project Management, QS, Erection & Commissioning, O & M, Quality , HSE and Shared Services.

Our Jobseekers represents talent from EPC, Construction, Power, Renewable Energy, Water, Environment, Roads & Highways, Telecom Infra, Cement, Transportation, Equipment Rental, Ports & Real Estate Sectors.


Founder


Balraj Verma Bidhuri is an MBA in HR and a Certified Recruitment Specialist with 17 yrs. in HR/ Talent Acquisition function.
He has worked closely with Top Business leaders, HR Leaders and Project Heads including Expatriates on Talent Acquisition & Strategy , Project HR, Organization structuring, Compensation, T&D, and other HR Challenges. He worked with Sterlite Power, SPML Infra, SREI- Quippo and HDFC Retail in Power, Water, Environment, Mining, Roads & Highways, Telecom Infra, Oil & Gas and Equipment Rental sector.

He founded MANFRA - 1st Infrastructure Manpower Company in India , to address unique Talent acquisition needs of EPC industry.
He is a Certified Yoga Trainer, registered with world Yoga Alliance and a life member of Sivananda Yoga, Rishikesh.
