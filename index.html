About us

We understand the Organization Structures specific to an Infrastructure Company, Project Work Flow, and Job profiles like BD, Tendering & Bidding, Design, Planning, SCM & Procurement, Construction, Project Management, QS, Erection & Commissioning, O & M, Quality , HSE and Shared Services.

Our Jobseekers represents talent from EPC, Construction, Power, Renewable Energy, Water, Environment, Roads & Highways, Telecom Infra, Cement, Transportation, Equipment Rental, Ports & Real Estate Sectors.


OUR TEAM

Founder

Balraj Verma Bidhuri is an MBA in HR and a Certified Recruitment Specialist with 17 yrs. in HR/ Talent Acquisition function. He has worked with Sterlite Grid, SPML Infra, SREI- Quippo and HDFC Retail in Power, Water, Environment, Mining, Roads & Highways, Telecom Infra, Oil & Gas and Equipment Rental sector.

He has worked closely with Top Business leaders, HR Leaders and Project Heads including Expatriates on Talent Acquisition & Strategy , Project HR, Organization structuring, Compensation, T&D, and other HR Challenges.

He is envisioned MANFRA to provide Right Manpower at the Right Time at the Right Cost to the Industry especially infra Start-Ups.

He is a Certified Yoga Trainer who loves to Travel and an avid Biker.
